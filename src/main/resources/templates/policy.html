1.6. Placing Orders.

1. Orders are carried out in the surrounding areas of individual Restaurants. Information about the possibility of performing the delivery to the address indicated by the User and the Restaurant performing the delivery are available on the Website and Application at the beginning of placing the Order. Checking the possibility of completing the Order by the appropriate Restaurant occurs automatically. If it is not possible to process the Order for the address entered, the User shall be notified of this fact by a message.
2. Payment for the Order may, at the User's choice, be made on delivery - in cash, by VISA or Master Card
3. Orders can be placed by both Users who have an Account and those who do not have an Account. In order to place an Order by a User who does not have an Account, such User should:
4. select the Order type on the Website or Application;
5. complete the following fields in the Order form: city, street and building number, mobile phone;
6. select the ordered products offered by the Restaurants on the Website by selecting them, choosing the number of pieces, quantity or size of the given product and making possible modifications to the given product, if such possibility has been provided for the given product;
7. select a payment method;
8. Accept the Order using the order confirmation button.
9. After completing all the steps described above, the Order is accepted for processing.
10. The Service Provider reserves the right to confirm by phone the fact of placing an Order using the telephone number provided by the User when placing the Order or creating an Account.
11. Orders may be placed by Users acting on behalf of other entities, including legal people and organizational units without legal personality.
12. After submitting the Order, the User may receive a VAT invoice covering the given Order.
